About Me

My name is Noam Weisberg, also known online as eternalStudent, and I am a programmer.

The software industry is broken, and I'm researching how we could do better. My current project is a visual assembler. It is not a solution, but a research project. Some screenshots are available here.

I also organize and host the Handmade Israel Meetups, a local chapter of the Handmade community, a community of programmers creating better software by using deep knowledge about how computer systems actually work. If you are interested subscribe here.
