About Me

My name is Noam Weisberg, also known online as eternalStudent. I've been programming since I was 10 years old, and writing software professionally for over a decade.

I'm currently studying the field of Mechanistic Interpretability in the hope of becoming a researcher.

I used to organize and host the Handmade Israel Meetups, a local chapter of the Handmade community, a community of programmers creating better software by using deep knowledge about how computer systems actually work. Currently on indefinite hiatus.

I wrote an interactive explainer about SDF Anti-Aliasing.

I also wrote notes about Set Theory as part of an idea about "Math from Scratch".
